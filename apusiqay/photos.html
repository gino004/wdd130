The tour

In this landscape we can appreciate the majestic place with an impressive view of the sun that is at sunset, as if that were not enough, you can take pictures of the light that the sun emits and show the beauty of nature. It is good to go with a group of friends to be able to see this sighting, which is approximately from 3pm to 5pm in the afternoon.

Sunset

In this part you can see another photo where it can be shown at an angle of the posted image, taking a good camera with good resolution you can get very good images from that height as shown.

Camping

Near the statue of the fog catcher there is a place where you can camp, during the night you can see the stars in an image never seen before since at that height we can see the night sky more clearly, and it is recommended to be able to do camping between the months of July and September.

Clouds

In this part we can appreciate the clouds of this image, the clouds are appreciated very well in this place to be able to take good images and that is because the sunset between 3pm and 4pm and these paradisiacal clouds can be captured along the hike.

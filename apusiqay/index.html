Apu Siqay

The cloud mattress

Located in El Triángulo, between three districts of Lima, visitors will be fascinated by a completely clear panoramic view of Lima, depending on the season of the year in which they visit the summits of this tourist circuit.

In the heights of southern Lima, between the districts of Villa María del Triunfo, La Molina and Pachacámac, there is a place where the sky seems to be closer. This is Apu Siqay, located in El Triángulo, named for the confluence of these three districts. It is there, among the highest peaks of Metropolitan Lima, that a beautiful cushion of clouds or a completely clear panoramic view of the capital can be seen, depending on the season of the year. Those interested in sightseeing without leaving the city can visit it any day of the week and on their own. All you need is time, a few suns and a good physical condition.
